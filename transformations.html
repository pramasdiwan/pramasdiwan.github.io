Case Study

Sneha R.

Working Mother of Two, Pune · 12 Weeks Program

-7 kg Lost
-4.5 in Waist
12 Weeks

Where She Started

Sneha came to coaching after trying four different diets in two years, each one ending in regained weight. She had 30–40 minutes a day, no gym access, and a deep distrust of fitness advice after repeated disappointment.

The Program

91 Days Health Mastery Program — home calisthenics 5x/week paired with a flexible, rice-and-roti-inclusive meal plan, with weekly check-ins to adjust around her work travel.

The Result

Lost 7 kg and 4.5 inches off her waist in 12 weeks, while reporting more energy at work than she'd had in years. She's continued independently for 6 months past the program with no regain.

Anjali M.

New Mother, Bengaluru · 16 Weeks Program

-6.5 kg Lost
-5 in Waist
16 Weeks

Where She Started

Eight months postpartum, Anjali couldn't recognize her body in photos and had been told generic gym programs weren't realistic with a newborn at home.

The Program

Calisthenics For Moms, with pelvic-floor-safe core work and sessions built around her baby's nap schedule, plus a postpartum-appropriate nutrition plan.

The Result

Rebuilt full-body strength from scratch, lost 6.5 kg, and reported feeling stronger than before her pregnancy — with energy to spare for her baby in the evenings.

Homemaker, Jaipur · 18 Weeks Program

-11 kg Lost
-7 in Waist
18 Weeks

Where She Started

Meenal had struggled with weight for over a decade and felt fitness content online was never built for someone starting from her level.

The Program

An extended 91 Days Health Mastery cycle with a slow, joint-friendly progression and a heavily personalized nutrition plan around her family's home cooking.

The Result

Lost 11 kg and 7 inches off her waist over 18 weeks — the largest sustained transformation in this case study set, achieved without a single crash diet phase.
